BIRT Example Reports

A Taste of what you can do with BIRT

Introduction

The example reports here show just a few of the things you can do with BIRT. These reports are based on a sales forecast database for MultiChip Technologies, a small chip vendor.

Simple Lists

These examples show a simple tabular listing.
Grouped List (design)
A simple tabular listing with two levels of grouping. This example is for a list of MultiChip's customers, sorted by state and city.
Grouped List with "Drop" Headings
BIRT allows you to merge group headers onto the same table row as the first detail row. This example is also for a list of MultiChip's customers, sorted by state and city.

Side-by-Side Layout

Side-by-Side Subreports (design) (notes)
This listing information about a MultiChip employee. It gives the summary information for the employee (name, address, etc.) along with side-by-side listings of the employee's job history on the left, and salary history on the right.

Business Forms

Invoice (design)
This is a typical invoice with sections for the customer, order and order line items. This example illustrates a simple computed column (extension), and a simple total.
